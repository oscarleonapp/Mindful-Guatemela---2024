About

Mindful Guatemala

Mindfulness and meditation are powerful tools. They bring calmness and balance to our lives and help us cope with stressful and challenging situations.


At Mindful Guatemala, we are passionate about sharing these benefits with people from all backgrounds.


We offer both social and private programs for all ages to learn mindfulness skills that help integrate social, emotional and mental well-being. Join us in creating a holistic and sustainable society of peace for all.
